🔒 Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains clearly how NocturnaVoyage collects, uses, and protects user data in accordance with applicable laws.

NocturnaVoyage is an independent digital project focused on travel guides, custom NFTs, and nightlife content.

✔️ Purpose of data processing
- To respond to information or collaboration requests.
- To improve the content and services offered.
- To anonymously analyze traffic and improve the user experience.
- To display personalized ads via advertising partners.

✔️ Legal basis
Data processing is based on user consent or the legitimate interest of the site owner to provide requested services.

✔️ Data sharing
We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share anonymized data with analytics and advertising providers (e.g., Google).

✔️ Data retention
We keep data only as long as necessary to respond to requests or meet legal obligations.

✔️ Third-party services
We use Google Analytics and Google AdSense, which may set cookies to track browsing preferences anonymously or pseudonymously.

✔️ Child protection
This site is intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18 years of age.

✔️ User rights
You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by writing to: nocturnavoyage@gmail.com.

✔️ Policy updates
This policy may be updated without notice. We encourage you to check it periodically.
